Welcome to the Golden Depths: Wumpus World Game

Objective: You are trapped in a dark maze and must navigate through the rooms to find the gold, all while avoiding dangerous pitfalls and the deadly Wumpus.

How to Play:

You control your character using the arrow buttons. Your goal is to:

  • Find the gold and escape the maze.
  • Avoid the Wumpus, a dangerous creature that can eat you if you're in the same room.
  • Watch out for pits, which can cause you to fall to your death if you enter a room containing one.

Sensory Clues:

You will receive sensory clues about your surroundings:

  • Stench: Indicates that the Wumpus is in an adjacent room. (You’ll see the letter S)
  • Breeze: Indicates that there’s a pit in an adjacent room. (You’ll see the letter B)
  • Glitter: Indicates that the gold is in an adjacent room. (You’ll see the letter G)

Game Controls:

Use the following buttons to control your movement and actions:

  • Move: Use the Up, Down, Left, or Right buttons to move to an adjacent room.
  • Shooting: If you have arrows left, you can shoot in the direction you're currently facing to try and hit the Wumpus.

Rules:

  • Wumpus: If you move into the room where the Wumpus is, it will eat you and you lose the game.
  • Pits: If you move into a room containing a pit, you fall to your death and lose the game.
  • Gold: If you find the gold, you win the game and escape the maze.
  • Arrows: You have 3 arrows to use. Be careful when shooting because they are limited!
