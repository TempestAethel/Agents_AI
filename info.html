Golden Depths: Wumpus World

Welcome to Golden Depths: Wumpus World! In this thrilling game, you find yourself trapped in a dark maze, filled with dangerous creatures and hidden treasures. Can you navigate your way to victory, or will the Wumpus catch you first? Read the instructions below to get started!

Objective:

Your goal is simple but dangerous:

  • Find the gold hidden somewhere in the maze.
  • Avoid the Wumpus, a terrifying creature that can eat you if you enter the room it's in.
  • Watch out for pits, which can cause you to fall to your doom.

How to Play:

You control your character using the arrow buttons on the screen. Here are the basic steps:

  • Move: Use the Up, Down, Left, or Right buttons to move between adjacent rooms.
  • Shooting: You have a limited number of arrows. Use the "Shoot Arrow" button to shoot in the direction you're facing and try to hit the Wumpus.

Sensory Clues:

As you explore, you'll receive sensory clues that help you navigate the maze:

  • Stench (S): Indicates that the Wumpus is in an adjacent room. Stay away!
  • Breeze (B): Indicates that thereâ€™s a pit nearby. Be cautious!
  • Glitter (G): Indicates that the gold is in an adjacent room. Follow the glitter!

Game Rules:

  • The Wumpus: If you enter the room with the Wumpus, it will eat you, and you lose the game.
  • The Pits: Stepping into a room with a pit means you fall to your death and lose the game.
  • The Gold: If you find the gold, you win the game and escape the maze!
  • Arrows: You have 3 arrows to use. If you run out of arrows, you're out of luck!

Good luck, adventurer. The maze is dark and treacherous, but with careful planning and a little bit of luck, you may just make it out alive!
